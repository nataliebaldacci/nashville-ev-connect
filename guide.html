Code Definitions

Electric Vehicle Charging Station

Equipment that is connected to the electrical grid or on-site power generation and is designed to charge electric vehicles. Classified as a utility accessory use under the zoning code.

Source: ¬ß 17.04.060 (proposed)

Electric Vehicle Charging Facility

A principal commercial use where EV charging is the primary purpose of the site. Includes standalone charging plazas and depots.

Source: ¬ß 17.04.060

Level 2 EVSE

Electric vehicle charging equipment with an output between 3 kW and 22 kW using alternating current (AC).

Source: BL2025-1116 substitute ordinance

DCFC (Direct Current Fast Charger)

Electric vehicle charging equipment with a capacity of 50 kW or more using direct current (DC).

Source: BL2025-1116 substitute ordinance
